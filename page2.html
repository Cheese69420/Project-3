One of My Favorite Songs: Eminem - Cleaning Out My Closet

"Cleaning Out My Closet" by Eminem is one of my favorite songs. What I find particularly interesting about this track is the way Eminem openly confronts his past, especially his complex relationship with his mother. He mentions in the song that his mother even made a diss track against him, which caught my attention.
